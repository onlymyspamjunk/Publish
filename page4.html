Submiting a Request

If you need to submit a Service Request under warranty

Visit the developer's page and fill out the form at https://www.discoveryhomes.com/homeowner-warranty -> Submit Service Request

Generally you have one year for defects to be fixed under warranty

However, HOA has up to 10 years to dispute major defects with developer, in court most of such cases are easily won by HOA

Customer Service

Normal and Emergency Requests

If you have a normal Customer Service request or a question to the developer about warranty, defect and similar questions, you can call during normal business hours M-F 8-5 at 925-671-7711 (Seeno Homes Customer Service)

You can call the same number during and after business hours for emergency reasons, such as you being flooded, etc.

You can also call HOA any time of the day at the regular HOA contact number (listed here in HOA section) if you have maintenance related question or an emergency issue, such as you got locked out and cannot get inside the gate because the gate is not working, etc.
